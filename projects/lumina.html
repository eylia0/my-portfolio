Lumina Challenge — Seat of Collaboration

An interactive stool designed for Sydney VIVID, celebrating unity through play.

Experience Public Interaction Sydney VIVID

Project intent

Lumina is designed to make collaboration physical. It disassembles into five parts, inviting participants to complete shared challenges. When reassembled, the stool illuminates — a symbol of connection and the spark created by teamwork.

Core idea

  • Disassembly invites interaction
  • Challenges require cooperation
  • Light rewards completion

Process (add as you go)

  • Research: public engagement + playful behavior in shared spaces
  • Sketch exploration: silhouette, assembly logic, stability
  • Prototyping: joinery tests + safe edges + durability
  • Interaction: how strangers understand “what to do” instantly

Outcome

A minimal form with an unusual behavior — the object becomes complete through people, not instructions.
